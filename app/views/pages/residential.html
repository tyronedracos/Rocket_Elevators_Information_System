Welcome to Rocket Elevators residential services. The Rocket Home Elevator is an innovative residential elevator which can help you stay in the home you love when the stairs become more difficult. Our selection of elevator home lifts are a practical and popular alternative to a traditional stairlift. Rocket home lifts are much easier and less expensive to install than a traditional hydraulic home elevator.

Rocket : Four Areas of Residential Expertise

QUIET:
Unlike other home elevators, Rocket do not use noisy hydraulics or vacuum systems as our home lifts are powered by a quiet built-in electric system. It simply plugs straight into a regular outlet and does not require a machine room or external pump system.

POWER CONSUMPTION:
All Rocket elevators residential elevators are very efficient and use a small amount of power. The home lift plugs straight into a standard domestic power socket making the home lift low on power consumption and very economical.

INSTALLATION:
Our residential elevators are made up of modular sections which all capable of passing through and under standard doorways and ceilings. This drastically reduces installation time and means a standard retrofit application takes no more than one day.

GUIDE SYSTEME:
The two parallel and vertical guides bear the load of the entire residential elevator. As the guides are at the centre line of the lift they create a self-supporting structure which eliminates the need for supporting walls.

Rocket Home will be nice in my house?

At Rocket, our stylish residential elevators offer homeowners an ideal way of future proofing their property with a more convenient way of travelling between floors. Choosing a Rocket Home Elevator is a more cost-effective compared to moving house and less expensive and disruptive than building a downstairs extension – and by adding a home lift you can add value to your home.

  • Small Footprint
  • Self Contained Drive System

The Rocket Home Elevator has one of the smallest footprints of any residential lift available and takes up less than 7sf. It can be placed at any angle and the remarkably small footprint means there are almost limitless spaces where our lift can be installed.

The Rocket Home Elevator uses a roped drum-braked gear motor drive system which is neatly contained within the lift itself. All of the mechanics are in one place, hidden from view, situated at the top of the in-home elevator.
